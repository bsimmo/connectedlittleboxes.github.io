Electronics

Electronics

Our boxes don't contain a lot of hardware. Most of the time an external device is just connected directly to the pins of whatever processor you are using. However, it is useful to know which pin you wire to which device. We have a standard circuit configuration for boxes and connected peripherals which you can see on the right. Larger verions are bundled in with the hardware printable designs.

Get the circuits

You can find the circuit diagrams nestling snugly alongside our hardware designs which you can find here.

We will be adding a bill of materials for each device, along with build instructions.

Flexibility Built In

Of course, there's nothing stopping you from doing your own thing. If you absolutely must connect your leds to GPIO6 then you can do that, and then just configure our software to tell it to use a that pin. And don't feel constrained to build boxes like ours either. If you want to make a box that does everything, or just one thing, then go for it.

Every box can be custom configured for its role and you can change the configuration any time you like, if you find a use for a few more pins..
